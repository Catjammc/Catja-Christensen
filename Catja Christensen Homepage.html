Catja Christensen is a writer, dancer, and dance
scholar from Virginia, USA. Currently based in
London, she earned an MRes in Dance
Choreography and Performance from the
University of Roehampton as a Fulbright Scholar in
2024.


She has written for Pointe Magazine, OOF
Magazine, thINKingDANCE, The Oslo Desk, and
Connecticut College’s The College Voice.

She also works in arts administration for The Royal
Ballet School and Gold Standard Arts Foundation.
